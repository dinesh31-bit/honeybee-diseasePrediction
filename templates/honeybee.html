Honeybee Disease Prediction

About Project

The project was made using Python, TensorFlow, Flask for backend development, and HTML/CSS. We took 2 classes and named them as infected honeybee and healthy honeybee. The data was divided into training and testing, with 70% for training and 30% for testing. We used a CNN model to train the images and saved the model as keras_model.h5.
